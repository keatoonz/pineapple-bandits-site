Bio

Pineapple Bandits are an electrifying four-piece alternative rock band hailing from [Your City]. Known for their energetic live shows, catchy hooks, and a penchant for pineapple-themed shenanigans, they've been making waves in the indie scene since 2022.

Band Members

  • Jamie "Juice" Rivera – Vocals / Guitar
  • Casey "Crush" Ling – Bass
  • Morgan "Spikes" Lee – Drums
  • Avery "Slice" Kim – Guitar / Keys

About

Blending influences from punk, surf, and classic alternative, Pineapple Bandits deliver a unique sound that’s both nostalgic and fresh. Their debut EP, "Fruit Fight," is available on all streaming platforms.
